## Dosage & Usage Guide

### Whey / Plant Protein
- Typical serving: 1 scoop (~30g) providing 24–25g protein
- Best taken post-workout, or anytime to meet daily protein goals
- Mix with 200–250ml water or milk
- General daily protein target: ~1.6–2.2g per kg bodyweight (from all sources)

### Creatine Monohydrate
- Standard dose: 3–5g per day, every day (including rest days)
- Optional loading phase: 20g/day (split into 4 doses) for 5–7 days, then 5g/day
- Timing does not matter much — consistency is what counts
- Drink plenty of water through the day

### Pre-Workout
- Take 1 scoop with water 20–30 minutes before training
- Contains caffeine — avoid taking within 6 hours of bedtime
- Start with half a scoop to assess tolerance
- Do not exceed 1 scoop in a 24-hour period

### BCAA / EAA
- Mix 1 scoop with water, sip during or after workouts
- Useful for intra-workout hydration and recovery, especially when training fasted

### Vitamins (Multivitamin, D3, Omega-3, Vitamin C)
- Take with a meal for best absorption (especially fat-soluble vitamins like D3 and Omega-3)
- Follow the serving size on the label — do not exceed unless advised by a doctor

## FAQs

**Q: How do I place an order?**
A: Order directly on spicenutrition.in or message us here on WhatsApp! Just tell us the product, flavour, size, and your delivery address.

**Q: When is the best time to take protein?**
A: Post-workout is popular, but what matters most is hitting your total daily protein. You can have it any time that helps you reach your goal.

**Q: Do I need to "load" creatine?**
A: Loading (20g/day for a week) saturates your muscles faster, but it's optional. Taking 3–5g daily reaches the same level in about 3–4 weeks.

**Q: Are your products vegan?**
A: Our Plant Protein and several vitamins are 100% vegan. Whey-based proteins are vegetarian (contain milk derivatives). Vegan options are clearly labelled.

**Q: Is whey protein safe? Any side effects?**
A: For most healthy adults, whey is safe. Some people with lactose sensitivity may prefer our isolate or plant protein. If you have a medical condition, please consult your doctor first.

**Q: Are your supplements FSSAI approved?**
A: Yes! Every product is FSSAI-approved and third-party lab-tested. The license number is printed on each label.

**Q: Will pre-workout keep me awake?**
A: It contains caffeine, so avoid taking it within ~6 hours of sleep. We also have a stim-free pump pre-workout if you train late.

**Q: Is this product authentic / not fake?**
A: 100% authentic, sourced directly from our GMP-certified facility. Every tub has a verification code and tamper-proof seal.

**Q: Can I take multiple supplements together (stacking)?**
A: Yes, many supplements stack well (e.g., protein + creatine). See our stacking tips below, and ask us for a recommendation based on your goal!

**Q: Do you offer products for weight loss / muscle gain?**
A: Yes — for muscle gain, look at our proteins and mass gainer; for lean goals, our isolate and BCAAs. Tell us your goal and budget and we'll recommend a stack.

**Q: What if my size/flavour is out of stock?**
A: We can add you to the waitlist and notify you on WhatsApp the moment it's restocked!

**Q: Can minors / pregnant women use these?**
A: We recommend supplements for adults 18+. If you are pregnant, nursing, or under 18, please consult a physician before use.
